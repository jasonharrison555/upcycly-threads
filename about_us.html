About Me

Hi, I’m Mariya Harrison! With a degree in Apparel Design, my journey has always been about more than just creating beautiful clothing, it’s about making a positive impact. I have a deep love for sewing, and there’s something magical about transforming fabric into something new and meaningful. But my passion doesn’t stop there.

I’m also dedicated to helping the planet. I believe in the power of upcycling and repurposing materials to reduce waste and promote sustainability. Each project I take on is an opportunity to create something unique, eco-friendly, and one-of-a-kind.

Whether I’m sewing a custom piece, teaching a new skill, or giving new life to old materials, I’m driven by the desire to create with purpose. Thank you for joining me on this journey to make the world a little more beautiful, sustainable, and fun!
