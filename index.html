Why Upcycle?

There are plenty of old and unwanted materials out there being thrown away, filling up our landfills and polluting our earth. One of the solutions to this problem is upcycling. You take those old and unwanted materials and instead of throwing them away, you turn them into something new. Helping protect the world one upcycled creation at a time.

What makes us unique?

Eco-Friendly

We give fabrics a second life by upcycling clothes and turning them into imaginative costumes and toys.

Handmade

Every product is handmade with tender love and care.

Durable and Safe

Our upcycled materials are handpicked carefully to ensure durability and cleanliness so children can enjoy them for years to come.

Our mission

Our mission is to help keep the environment safe while also helping provide kids with adorable toys and costumes to enjoy!
